About me

Hello! My name is Peter Maila, I am an electrical engineer and a self-taught front-end web developer.
I am a passionate web developer with practical experience in developing websites using HTML5, CSS3, and Bootstrap. I have a keen eye for detail and strive to create visually appealing and user-friendly websites. My love for technology and constant desire to learn and improve has helped me sharpen my skills in these frameworks.
I am also the CEO and founder of Flavory, where we build world-class digital ads and assets

I follow the three pillars—design, develop, and deploy— that form the foundation of my approach to website development

Design

I prioritize the power of captivating design. I believe that a visually appealing website is essential to grab the attention of visitors and create a lasting impression. I am skilled in creating modern, clean, and user-friendly interfaces that engage users and enhance their browsing experience. I pay attention to every detail, from color schemes and typography to layout and navigation, ensuring that the design reflects the unique identity and goals of each client's business

Develop

With my strong foundation in HTML5, CSS3, and Bootstrap, I develop fully responsive websites. I bring ideas to life, turning design concepts into fully responsive websites that are optimized for performance, accessibility, and compatibility across different devices and browsers. I follow best practices and coding standards to ensure clean, efficient, and maintainable code

Deploy

I understand that a successful website is not just about design and development—it's about seamless deployment and a smooth user experience. My deployment process focuses on delivering websites that are secure, reliable, and ready for action. I conduct thorough testing to identify and resolve any potential issues before launch. I also provide hosting and domain management services.
